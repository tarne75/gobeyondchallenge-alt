Beat the Sunset 5K

A super quick 5K road race on a Sunday evening in Northamptonshire. Like a fun social event open to individuals and club runners alike. Beat the Sunset is the perfect summer evening event — fast, fun, and sociable.

About the Event

Whether you're chasing a PB or just want a fun evening run with friends, this event is for you. Open to all abilities, Beat the Sunset combines competitive racing with the social atmosphere of a summer evening event. Race against the clock and the setting sun in this fast and friendly 5K.
