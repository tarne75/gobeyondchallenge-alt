The Shires & Spires Running Festival

Five distances for everyone in beautiful Naseby, Northamptonshire

The Shires & Spires Running Festival

Five distances for everyone — 10K, Half Marathon, Three-Quarter Marathon (new for 2024), Marathon and 35-mile Ultra. Based in the village of Naseby, famous for the Battle of Naseby in 1645. The Half Marathon is the winner of the MARM Race of the Year 2023.

About the Event

Picture this — you're stood on the start line in a beautiful Northamptonshire village and everyone is a little apprehensive about what's to come. But hey you are all running, some further than others, but the excitement and anticipation is the same for everyone. A five-distance circular event with a mixture of trails and roads.

Choose your distance and experience the festival atmosphere with runners of all abilities, from first-time 10K runners to seasoned ultra marathoners, all celebrating together in the stunning Northamptonshire countryside.
