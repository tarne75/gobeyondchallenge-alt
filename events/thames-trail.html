Thames Trail Ultra

48 miles along two of the country's most beautiful National Trails

Thames Trail Ultra

Forty-eight miles along the Thames from Oxford to Henley on two of the country's National Trails — the Thames Path and the Ridgeway. Admire the stunning countryside views, stunning houses, beautiful locks and traditional rowing clubs along the route.

About the Event

This is a point-to-point race following the Thames Path and Ridgeway National Trails. The route passes through some of England's most beautiful riverside towns and villages. It's an iconic ultra running experience showcasing some of the best of southern England's countryside and heritage.

Grand Slam eligible — complete all four UK ultras for a discounted entry and the ultimate challenge.
