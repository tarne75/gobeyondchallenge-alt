Rose of the Shires Ultra & Rosebud Marathon

54 miles (Rose) or 26.2 miles (Rosebud) through beautiful Northamptonshire

Rose of the Shires Ultra & Rosebud Marathon

A choice of the 54-mile Rose or the 26.2 Rosebud taking you on a journey through beautiful Northamptonshire along country trails, roads and towpath. The route is approximately 70% trail and 30% on road.

About the Event

This is one of the most scenic runs in the East Midlands, taking you through beautiful Northamptonshire villages, past historical sites and through stunning countryside. The Rose is a 54-mile ultra marathon and the Rosebud is a 26.2-mile marathon distance, following the same course. Both are circular routes based in Northamptonshire.

Grand Slam eligible — complete all four UK ultras for a discounted entry and the ultimate challenge.
