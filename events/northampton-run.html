The Amazing Northampton Run

A 13.1-mile loop showcasing the best of Northampton

The Amazing Northampton Run

Join us for the fourth edition of the Amazing Northampton Run. A 13.1-mile loop around the town, starting at the historic Market Square, following the river past Northampton's three main sporting grounds, through three beautiful parks, passing through the modern University campus and returning to the Market Square.

About the Event

A brilliant town-centre half marathon showcasing the best of Northampton. This event combines the joy of running with a real community experience. Starting from the heart of the town and returning to it, you'll enjoy every aspect of what makes Northampton special — from its sporting heritage to its beautiful riverside parks and its vibrant University community.
