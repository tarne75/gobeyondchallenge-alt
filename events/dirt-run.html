Dirt Run — Irchester & Brigstock

Multi-terrain running through Northamptonshire's finest country parks

Dirt Run — Irchester & Brigstock

A multi-terrain 5k, 10k or 15k run around Irchester Country Park and 7.5k and 15k at Fermyn Woods Country Park. If you like things a little more… well dirty! A brilliant series of off-road runs set in some of Northamptonshire's finest country parks.

About the Event

Proudly supported by National Trust and Forestry England. The Dirt Run series is perfect for runners of all abilities looking for something a bit different. Mud, trails and brilliant fun! We are proud to be supporters of SheRACES, committed to bringing more women into running.

Round 1: 25 January 2026 (Irchester) · Round 2: 22 February 2026 (Brigstock)
