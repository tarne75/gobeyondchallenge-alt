Country to Capital Ultra Marathon

43 miles from Buckinghamshire countryside to Little Venice, London

Country to Capital Ultra Marathon

This 43-mile start to the ultra running season takes you through stunning Buckinghamshire countryside to picturesque Little Venice in London, taking in the varied sights of the capital's suburbs along the Grand Union Canal. One of the UK's favourite winter ultras.

About the Event

The route starts in Wendover, Buckinghamshire and follows the towpath of the Grand Union Canal for much of the route, finishing in Little Venice, a beautiful waterway area in West London. This is a point-to-point race with a coach pick-up at the start.

Why not do the Grand Slam? Complete all four UK ultras (Country to Capital, Rose of the Shires, Thames Trail, Shires & Spires) for a discounted entry and the ultimate challenge.
