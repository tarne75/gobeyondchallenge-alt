She Races

Encouraging greater female participation in endurance events

About She Races

We are very aware that ultramarathon events are often dominated by men, with a much smaller percentage of women making it to the start line. At Go Beyond Challenge we are striving to reduce the gender gap, encouraging more women to take part in long distance running by removing barriers to entry.

What We're Doing

Our commitment to supporting women in endurance sport includes:

  • Reduced entry fees for women participants
  • Female-only start waves available at selected events
  • Partnership with SheRACES and Women in Tri/Fund Her Tri UK
  • Dedicated women's podiums and prizes
  • Female marshals and support crew on route
  • Safe, inclusive environment for all participants

Our Partnership with SheRACES

We're proud to work alongside SheRACES, a community dedicated to inspiring and supporting women in endurance sport. Together, we're building a more inclusive endurance events landscape where women feel welcome, supported, and celebrated.
