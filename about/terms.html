Terms & Conditions for Go Beyond Challenge Events

Last updated: April 2026

1. Entry and Registration

By entering a Go Beyond Challenge event, you agree to be bound by these terms and conditions. Entry is open to all persons aged 18 years or over (or younger with parental consent as specified in the event guidelines). Entry closes on the date specified for each event and no entries will be accepted after this date unless specifically approved by Go Beyond Challenge Ltd. Entries are non-transferable unless prior permission is obtained from Go Beyond Challenge Ltd.

2. Medical Requirements

All participants must be in good health and fit enough to complete the event. Participants with pre-existing medical conditions should consult their GP before entering. By entering, you confirm that you are fit to participate and have disclosed any relevant medical conditions. Go Beyond Challenge Ltd reserves the right to refuse entry to any participant deemed unfit to complete the event. Participants under medical advice not to participate should not enter.

3. Entry Fees and Payment

Entry fees are as stated on the event page at the time of entry. Payment must be made in full at the point of entry. All entry fees include administration costs and are non-refundable unless otherwise stated. Early bird discounts, group discounts, and women's discounts are available as specified for each event.

4. Refunds and Transfers

Refunds are available only in the following circumstances: (a) cancellation of the event by Go Beyond Challenge Ltd; (b) withdrawal due to medical grounds, with supporting evidence; or (c) as otherwise specified in our refund policy. Requests for refunds must be made in writing to admin@gobeyondchallenge.co.uk. Standard refund processing takes 4-6 weeks. Transfers to other participants or alternate events may be arranged at the discretion of Go Beyond Challenge Ltd and are subject to a 10 administrative fee.

5. Route Changes and Cancellation

Go Beyond Challenge Ltd reserves the right to modify the route, reduce distances, or cancel the event in the event of adverse weather, poor ground conditions, safety concerns, or other circumstances beyond our control. Decisions to modify or cancel events will be communicated to participants as soon as practically possible. In the event of cancellation, participants will be offered entry to an alternative event or a full refund.

6. Liability and Risk

All participants undertake the events at their own risk. Participants acknowledge that endurance events carry inherent risks including but not limited to: physical injury, adverse weather, traffic hazards, and other unforeseen circumstances. By entering, participants accept these risks. Go Beyond Challenge Ltd will not be held liable for any injury, loss, or damage to persons or property, except where liability cannot be excluded by law. Participants are responsible for their own safety and should withdraw from the event if unwell or injured.

Participants must comply with all instructions given by race marshals and officials. Marshals are present to ensure participant safety and the smooth running of the event. Participants must respect road traffic laws and adhere to road closure notices. Go Beyond Challenge Ltd reserves the right to remove any participant from the event who poses a danger to themselves or others.
