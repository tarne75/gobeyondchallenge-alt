Thank You for Your Interest in Go Beyond Challenge

We love what we do and we never take it for granted. We work hard to put on amazing events for our running friends in Northants, the UK and beyond.

Encouraging Greater Female Participation

We are very aware that ultramarathon events are often dominated by men. At Go Beyond Challenge we are striving to reduce the gender gap, encouraging more women to take part in long distance running.
